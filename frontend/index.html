Help & FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about Aequitas and the Casper Buildathon submission.

How does Sandbox Demo Mode work? Sandbox Demo Mode maps your actions to a pre-funded local wallet emulation. Real Casper Wallet Mode connects directly to your Casper extension to sign deploys on the Testnet.
What does the "arg not valid" error mean? This occurs when an argument (like U512 quantity bytes) is incorrectly formatted. The current Aequitas core automatically runs serialization validators to ensure correct Little-Endian format before signatures are requested.
